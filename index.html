About Me


Hi, I am originally from Los Angeles, CA, but have been a transplant in New Hampshire since the summer of 2017. I like to explore the outdoors by hiking and kayaking. When I'm not outside, I like to spend my time crafting, which could be knitting, crocheting, felting, sewing, or another method I have picked up. I like to listen to audiobooks and podcasts while I craft.
